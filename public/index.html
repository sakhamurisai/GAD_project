About Our App

We are here to help you assess and track your mood patterns, not to judge. Our goal is to provide you with insights into your emotional well-being and offer personalized recommendations to support your mental health journey.

"Mental health is not a destination, but a process. It's about how you drive, not where you're going."

- Noam Shpancer, Ph.D.

This app is designed to help you understand your emotional patterns, track your mood changes, and discover what works best for your mental well-being. We believe that everyone deserves support in their mental health journey, and we're here to provide that support without judgment.
